Our Story

Deez Hotpot is a vibrant catering company specializing in authentic Nigerian traditional cuisine, bringing the rich flavors and cultural heritage of Nigeria to every event. From hearty stews and flavorful soups to sizzling grilled meats and freshly made Abula, our diverse menu is a true celebration of Nigerian culinary traditions. Whether you're planning a wedding, corporate event, family gathering, or any special occasion,

Deez Hotpot delivers a memorable dining experience with mouthwatering dishes prepared using the finest ingredients and time-honored recipes. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, exceptional customer service, and commitment to making your event unforgettable with the bold and delicious tastes of Nigeria.
